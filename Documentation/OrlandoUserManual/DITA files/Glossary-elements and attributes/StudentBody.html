STUDENTBODY

Definition, Placement, Relationships

This optional attribute attached to the element SCHOOL (within BIOGRAPHY > EDUCATION) records whether the school is single-sex or inclusive. This attribute (with values SINGLESEX and CO-ED) helps us to interpret the influence of single-sex education on women writers across historical periods.

Example 
 <P><SCHOOL INSTITUTION="DAYSCHOOL"
                INSTITUTIONLEVEL="PRIMARY" STUDENTBODY="SINGLESEX">Miss
                Hannah Spray's Ladies' Day School</SCHOOL></P>
Practice Issues

Remember this attribute and use whenever the gender makeup of a school is known.

Troubleshooting

The same body will have different attribute values at different dates. Many universities and colleges began as all-male, then gradually admitted women. This element and its attributes reflect this historical process.
