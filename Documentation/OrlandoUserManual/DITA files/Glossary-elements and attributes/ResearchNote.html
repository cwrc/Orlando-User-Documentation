RESEARCHNOTE

Definition, Placement, Relationships

Available throughout the textbase, this element is not to be confused with SCHOLARNOTE that will be readable by end users. RESEARCHNOTE contents will be seen only by the eyes of Orlandians. Contains comments from one team member to another; which should be prefaced by initials of the writer, and which are not visible in delivery. An Orlandian writing a new entry will may leave research notes for or receive them from another who is checking or revising; these can be deleted once implemented or decided against. Retain only notes that may be useful downstream, like exhaustive search for firm date reveals nothing or recommended visual for this writer. (Research notes containing the word “visual” are clues to project of illustrating Orlando.)

Practice Issues

If you encounter research notes that refer to completed actions of negligible importance, feel free to delete them. However, you will want to retain research notes that say things like this is a typo/no it's not because they may save us a step later on. Retain research notes that have substantive information or queries about sources or research processes. If in doubt, leave it in.
